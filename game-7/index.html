  • High Concept: Avoid angels and traps and try to reach the end of the level.
  • Theme: Escaping from Heaven.
  • Prototype Goal: This prototype is test the ability to create a map out of tiles, and test the viability of the different types of enemies that can be created.
    DA6: The goal of this prototype was to create a new level with the addition of a new enemy that chased the player, as well as new types of tiles.
    DA7: The goal of this prototype was to test the functionality of wall jumping, add moving resource bars to the UI, as well as to improve upon stage 1.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should experience the enjoyment of platformer games, and the excitement of reaching your goal by getting past any danger.
  • Gameplay: The goal of the game is to reach the door that will bring you back to earth by avoiding any traps and the angels sent to capture you.
  • Strategies: Players should try to watch for incoming bullets, and use the energy barrier accordingly. If there's a locked door then players should look out for a key so they can progress further.
    DA6: Players can jump to help prevent cracked platforms from deteriorating.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: The game takes place in the outskirts of heaven, where you find yourself trying to escape and return back to earth. Angels have to come to capture you and bring you back, but you're willing to do all you can to go back to the place you call home.
  • Target Audience: The target audience is anyone who likes platformer games.
  • Play Time: Around 2-5 minutes.
